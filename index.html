Who we are

Jaypee Industrial Corporation commenced operations in the year 1978 as dealers in galvanised wires and it's products. Over the years keeping in mind our core principle of CUSTOMER FIRST , we have gained immense popularity among agriculturists and dealers in South India .

Jaypee is synonymous with quality and value for money. From 2003, we have been appointed as exclusive distributors for Tata Steel for Tata Wiron galvanised wires and it's products for the state of Karnataka . We are proud to be catering to 600 + retailers and coperative societies in the state .
